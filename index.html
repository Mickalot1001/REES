About Us

Welcome to REESS, the Russian and Eastern European Student Society at Queen's University! We are a vibrant and inclusive community dedicated to bringing together students from Russian and Eastern European backgrounds, as well as anyone interested in our rich cultures and traditions. At REESS, we aim to create a welcoming environment where members can connect, share experiences, and celebrate their heritage. Whether you're looking to meet new friends, enjoy cultural events, or simply have a good time, REESS is the place for you. We organize a variety of exciting events throughout the year, including cultural festivals, movie nights, social gatherings, and more. Our goal is to foster a sense of belonging and provide opportunities to explore and enjoy the diverse cultures of Eastern Europe. Join us and become part of our growing community! Follow us on social media to stay updated on upcoming events and activities. We can't wait to meet you!
